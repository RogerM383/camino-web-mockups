why teams choose camino

Scale learning without scaling complexity.

Launch in minutes, not months

how it works

From idea to live training in three steps.

01

Build your learning path

Upload existing content, write new material, or let Camino's AI generate a first draft from your brief. Define the structure, set the sequence, add your branding.

02

Assign to the right people

Select individuals, teams, departments, or your whole org. Set deadlines if needed. Learners get notified and can start immediately — on any device, in any format.

03

Watch engagement happen

Track completion in real time. See what's working. Iterate on content without pulling the whole course. Your training gets better the more it's used.
