Bring your own content

Upload slides, articles, PDFs, video scripts, or an entire book. Camino ingests your material and structures it into a navigable learning experience — without stripping your voice.

AI-assisted, human-led

Camino's AI helps you generate structure, draft content gaps, and suggest formats — but you stay in the driver's seat. The final program is unmistakably yours.

Launch fast, iterate faster

Get your first program live in days, not months. Tweak content based on learner behavior, add new modules, or update materials without rebuilding from scratch.

See how your content lands

Get clear data on what's resonating, where learners spend more time, and what they skip. Turn engagement signals into better programs with every iteration.

Multiformat by default

Your program is automatically available as video, podcast, article, and AI conversation. Clients get to choose — and they're more likely to complete when they do.

Scale your impact without scaling your hours

Once your program is live, it runs. Deliver to 5 clients or 500 without proportionally more of your time. Camino is the leverage between your expertise and your reach.
