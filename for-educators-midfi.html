what camino brings to education

Designed by educators. Powered by what works.

Universal Design for Learning

Camino is built on UDL principles — multiple means of representation, engagement, and expression. Every learner gets a path that meets them where they are.

Instant curricula from your materials

Upload your lecture notes, course outline, or reading list and Camino builds a structured learning path. Hours of curriculum design become minutes.

AI that deepens understanding

Students can ask the AI tutor questions about any lesson — in the context of your curriculum. It's like office hours that never end, without adding to your workload.

Real insights, not just grades

See how students engage with content, not just whether they submitted. Understand what's confusing, what's resonating, and where to focus your teaching energy.

Higher engagement, better outcomes

When students can choose how they engage, they engage more. Format flexibility is one of the most reliable drivers of completion and retention in education research.

Works across institutions and formats

Camino works for traditional courses, hybrid learning, professional development, and continuing education. It fits where your institution is, not just where it's going.

how it works

From curriculum to live learning in three steps.

01

Build your course structure

Import your existing materials or start from a brief. Camino generates a structured learning path — modules, sequences, and format options — that you review and refine.

02

Assign to your students

Add your class or cohort and they're in. Each student gets the same curriculum, accessible in their preferred format — no logins to manage, no IT headaches.

03

Watch them learn

Track engagement across your class. See who's ahead, who needs a nudge, and which topics are generating the most questions. Use the data to teach better, not just test more.
